    InfoEnh: Towards Multimodal Sentiment Analysis via Information Bottleneck Filter and Optimal Transport Alignment
    LREC-COLING 2024

    Here we apply the information bottleneck and optimal transportation to effectively address two key challenges in multimodal sentiment analysis: information redundancy and contradictory modality sentiment.

    Syntax Matters: Towards Spoken Language Understanding via Syntax-Aware Attention
    EMNLP (Findings) 2023

    Here we consider syntax matter in SLU, improve it by injecting syntactic information into the attention module, and explore the fusion methods between the original module and the one with syntactic information.
